About Us

What matters to us

Here at DucksKenya we take pride in raising quality poultry while adhering to the highest standards of animal welfare. This not only makes for happy, healthy and productive birds, but also for satisfied customers and easy poultry management with great rewards.

Pick-Up information

As our products are live animals, we are unable to offer shipment via postal service or courier and operate on a pick-up only basis. Collection is possible anytime during our opening hours.
While you are at DucksKenya farm to collect your order, our members of staff will be more than happy to show you around, answer any additional questions you might have and offer advice on all things poultry.

Contact Us

Please get in touch with us for any queries regarding your order, pick-up or general product information.
We are also happy to offer advice before or after your purchase with us. If you can't find your questions answered in our Forum, please reach out and we'll be glad to help!
